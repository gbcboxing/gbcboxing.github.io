Brick Buffpunch and the GBC Takeover

In January 2022 the GBC came back from a long time of radio silence. It turned out that after the russian mafia attacks of 2022 the GBC had spiralled into an all out war with the russian mafia. Eventually they seiged the GBC's office in Wales, forcing the remaining staff members to escape at night. During this escape, Jabadobbins was shot by a russian mafia sniper and believed to be dead.

Brick Buffpunch assumed control of the company and led the remaining staff members to Fiji, where he established a new office. Once they got set up, Brick Buffpunch announced Buff Lads 2022.

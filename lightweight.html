The GBC Lightweight Belt

The GBC Lightweight Belt is the third-highest weight category belt in the GBC. Its weight limit is 61kg.

The belt is currently vacant after former Lightweight champion The Reaper stepped up to Cruiserweight.

The belt is due to be contested next by Quintin Steel and Cousin Clarence at The Beard And The Bull.

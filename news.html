Changes to the 100m Scoring System!

By Anthony Lizardgreen - 10/03/2023

The GBC has changed the way the 100m will be scored starting with Buff Lads 2023!

The new scoring system is called the Chud Bungus method! It uses an exponential equation to calculate scores in a way that we hope will be much more fair!

Date announced for The Beard And The Bull!

By Anthony Lizardgreen - 15/05/2022

We here at the GBC have finished negotiating with the owners of the Tondu Garden Arena and have finally set a date for the upcoming Lightweight clash - The Beard And The Bull.

The event will be held on Sunday, July 17th, 2022 at the Tondu Garden Arena.

Tickets are going on sale at the end of this week, with pay-per-view purchases available starting June 19th. Mark it in your diaries!

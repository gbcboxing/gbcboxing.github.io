Cousin Clarence is a competitor in the GBC who fights in the Lightweight division.

Cousin Clarence is ABSOLUTELY NOT A FEMALE because in the GBC there are NO GIRLS ALLOWED. Cousin Clarence is a man, which you can tell by his beard.

Clarence is a rather mysterious competitor in the GBC, having only ever tweeted once. From what we can tell he lives a life of buff manliness in the wilderness somewhere, maybe alaska or something like that. Wherever it is he probably doesn't have internet access.

Buff Lads 2022

Cousin Clarence was a surprise entrant to Buff Lads 2022. He turned up and just started being buff with no warning. This has widely been considered a chad move.

Clarence's debut performance at Buff Lads earned him the silver medal in the plank, and the bronze medal in the deadlift. Some rather buff scores indeed.

The Beard And The Bull

On May 8th, Clarence issued a challenge for the vacant GBC Lightweight belt. This fight was booked as The Beard And The Bull.

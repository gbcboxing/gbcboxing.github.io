The GBC Flyweight Belt

The GBC Flyweight Belt is the fourth-highest weight category belt in the GBC. Its weight limit is 48kg.

The current champion is Rock Kickass, who gained the belt when it was vacated by Quintin Steel's move up to Lightweight.

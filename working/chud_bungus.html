What is Chud Bungus?

The Chud Bungus method is a new approach for calculating 100m scores starting with Buff Lads 2023.

The Rationale

The previous scoring method was linear - you would score a point for each quarter of a second below the scoring threshold.

The problem with this approach is that it was unfair to speedy runners. Shaving a quarter of a second off your 12 second time should be worth more points than shaving a quarter of a second off your 25 second time.

We want to reward buff running and incentivise the 100m as a viable path to being the buffest lad!

Does This Affect Buff Lads 2019/2022?

No, the scores from Buff Lads 2019 and 2022 are not going to be changed. This only affects scores from Buff Lads 2023 onwards.

How Does it Work?

The Chud Bungus equation is:

Score = 6860000 * Seconds ^ -4.13

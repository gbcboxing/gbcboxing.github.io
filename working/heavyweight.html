The GBC Heavyweight Belt

The GBC Heavyweight Belt is the highest weight category belt in the GBC. Its has no weight limit.

The belt is currently vacant after former Heavyweight champion Jabadobbins was presumed dead and disqualified from competition.

The belt is unified with the Cruiserweight belt, but since there was no clear winner at The Riser of the Dead, it remains vacant.

The GBC Cruiserweight Belt

The GBC Cruiserweight Belt is the second-highest weight category belt in the GBC, with a maximum weight of 91kg.

The current champion is Bagstock, who is in his first reign.

The Cruiserweight belt is currently officially unified with the Heavyweight belt. The winner of The Riser of the Dead was supposed to become unified champion, but since that fight ended in a draw, no unified champion was crowned. Therefore even though the belts are supposed to be unified, the Heavyweight belt remains vacant.

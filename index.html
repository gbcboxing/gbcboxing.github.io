What We Do

The GBC (Garden Boxing Championship) is the world's top professional boxing organisation. Elite boxers from all around the globe compete to earn our belts.

We organise Buff Lads, the world's most elite strongman competition. You have to be incredibly buff to compete in this.

We also organise Buff Laddies, the world's premiere highland games. Any scotsman worth his tartan dreams of competing at this prestigious event.
